Features

Customer

Browse through the list of multiple stores pick your items and send a request. You can also view the progress of your order thanks to the map which shows the exact location of the driver completing your order.

Driver

Shop for multiple clients at once. Travel from store to store and fulfill the orders, then deliver them. Get paid for multiple orders at once!

App

Our app keeps track of all orders and money transfers. Preschedule orders and keep up to date with live updates of your drivers progress.

Goals

We will grow interest in our venture with advertisements, word of mouth, and promotions. This low overhead model will facilitate quick and controlled growth. All this, while helping local businesses increase sales and employing drivers. Possible growth opportunities lie in new demographics as well as new markets.

We aim to improve the quality of life of our clients and drivers. The ease of shopping online is mixed with instant and fresh delivery of goods; stimulating the local economy and offereing flexible jobs to those in need.
