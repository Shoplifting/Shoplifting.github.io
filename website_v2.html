How it Works


Being a Customer

Fastest Way to do Groceries

A couple of taps and you are able to shop for groceries. Our shoplyft drivers will know exactly what you want and will deliver your groceries right to your doorstep, within an hour.

Extremely Convenient

Whenever you feel like cooking, a Shoplyft courier will always be ready to deliver your groceries. Using the Shoplyft app you can order groceries anytime, any household, on all smartphone devices


Being a Driver

Do Groceries, Earn Money!

AAs an employee of Shoplyft you will be able to earn money, by obtaining groceries and delivering them to a location. You can start at anytime, and end at anytime. You'll be earning $5.50 + $1.00 per kilometer + tips every delivery, with a possiblity of earning more during high traffic times.

Extremely Convenient

It is the grocers resposibility to pick the items, however it is yours to arrive at the right store, and deliver them to the right location. As a driver you are able to set up your pick up range, so you don't have to travel huge distances.
